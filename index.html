ION revolutionizes the way we work

ION is the first all-in-one digital workplace for the efficient delivery of federal and commercial projects. ION breaks down large requirements and enables rapid procurement of services and work products directly from small businesses and individuals.

The problem we solve

The current infrastructure does not exist to allow mega projects and employers to use flexible workers efficiently. At the federal level, there are security, reputation and fair competition requirements that make flexi work models difficult.

ION Take Charge – is a complete digital workplace. Designed from the ground up to allow the efficient integration of flexi and contingent work models into mega project fulfilment.
