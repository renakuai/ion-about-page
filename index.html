ION revolutionizes the way we work

ION is the first all-in-one digital workplace for the efficient delivery of federal and commercial projects. ION breaks down large requirements and enables rapid procurement of services and work products directly from small businesses and individuals.

Our solution

The current infrastructure does not exist to allow mega projects and employers to use flexible workers efficiently.

ION Take Charge – is a complete digital workplace that has been designed from the ground up to allow the efficient integration of flexi and contingent work models into mega project fulfillment.

For the vendor

  • ION uses ML to make sure you receive notifications for suitable work
  • Easily bid for entire projects or suitable elements of larger campaigns
  • Track your reputation in an objective manner – one bad client cannot ruin your career
  • With ION your bids will be based on work products not your time – allowing you to reuse elements of existing work

For the employer

  • Advanced Machine Learning breaks massive projects into manageable work requirements
  • Receive instant bids from vetted professionals and ML selects the best bid
  • Built-in DevOps automates work delivery and management
  • Reduce time to task completion from months to days or even hours
